What is ARA?

Scope determination, without the guesswork

Before any security assessment can begin, the team needs to agree on what gets tested. That scoping process is traditionally manual, time-consuming, and prone to gaps — especially when assessors lack full context on an asset.

ARA replaces ad-hoc scoping conversations with a structured 5-section questionnaire. You answer questions about your asset's type, criticality, data classification, compliance obligations, and technical environment. ARA's engine evaluates your answers against 14 risk dimensions and returns a mandatory/recommended test scope — instantly.

The scope report is sent directly to your security team, so they have everything they need before the assessment even begins.

Structured Questionnaire

Five focused sections covering asset identity, criticality, technical profile, data types, and existing controls. Takes under 10 minutes to complete.

14 Risk Dimensions

From external penetration testing and OWASP API Top 10 through to DPIA, cloud security, and BC/DR — every relevant test area is evaluated automatically.

Delivered to Your Team

Results are sent directly to the security team by email — with mandatory and recommended test areas, priority ratings, and the rationale for each.
